About Me

I’m a product designer, passionate about shaping how people interact with products and experiences.

Alongside design, I’m a frontend developer, artist, musician, and Venture Partner @ Contrary.

The most interesting mixture of mediums to create interaction among people for me lies between how digital and real life experiences combine. I’m currently exploring the combination of visual and sound design.

I study Information Sciences & Technology at Penn State with a focus on Digital Art & Media, and teach a class on Digital Product Design. Recently, I lived in The Netherlands studying studio art & humanities at the Gerrit Rietveld Academie and University of Amsterdam.
